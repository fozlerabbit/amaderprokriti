About Us

Amader Prokriti is a youth-driven initiative dedicated to building climate-resilient communities through education, action, and collaboration. We believe that sustainable change begins with empowered individuals who understand their role in protecting our planet.

Our programs focus on practical solutions that make a real difference in communities, from waste management to green skill development.

Vision

We envision a world where every community has the knowledge, resources, and commitment to live sustainably. Our vision is to create a network of empowered youth leaders who drive environmental action, inspire policy change, and build resilient ecosystems that can thrive for generations to come. Through education, innovation, and collective action, we strive to make sustainability not just a goal, but a way of life.

Mission

  • Educate communities on climate change impacts and sustainable solutions
  • Implement practical waste reduction and recycling programs
  • Develop green skills training for youth employment opportunities
  • Foster partnerships between communities, organizations, and policymakers
  • Advocate for environmental policies that protect our natural resources
  • Create measurable impact through evidence-based interventions
